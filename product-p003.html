Description

American black walnut is one of the finest hardwoods in the world — rich in colour, tight in grain, and extraordinarily durable. We mill each tray from a single piece of solid timber, then hand-sand through 220 grit before finishing with food-safe pure tung oil. The oil soaks in rather than sitting on top, so it never chips or peels.
